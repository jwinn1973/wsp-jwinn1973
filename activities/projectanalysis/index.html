Site’s Intent

The website re-design of St. Francis Hospital for animals streamline the current 12-page design into a cohesive, user friendly 6-page who’s intent is to inform prospective customers of the services offered, hours of operation, and business locations.

The site re-design will address the improper html code and the amount of clutter (oversized jpegs, broken links etc.) A complete facelift is needed for a made updated look and user experience.
